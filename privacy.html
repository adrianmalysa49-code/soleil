Privacy Policy

Soleil — Your AI Friend

Last updated: April 2026

Soleil is an emotional support application. Your privacy is our priority. Below we describe what data we collect, how we use it and what rights you have.

👤 Data Controller

The data controller is Adrian Małysa, an individual operating the Soleil service, available at soleil-sage.vercel.app.

Contact: adrianmalysa49@gmail.com

📋 What data we collect

  • Account data: email address, name (optional) — upon registration via Clerk
  • Conversation history: content of your conversations with Soleil — stored in Supabase
  • Mood journal: daily mood ratings and optional notes
  • Subscription data: Premium payment status (via Stripe) — no card data
  • Technical data: IP addresses, browser type — standard server logs

🎯 Purpose of processing

  • Providing Soleil application services
  • Remembering conversation history and moods
  • Processing Premium payments (Stripe)
  • Ensuring security and preventing abuse

🔒 How we protect your data

  • Conversations stored in Supabase encrypted database
  • Login via Clerk — we don't store passwords
  • Payment card data handled exclusively by Stripe — we have no access to it
  • Connections encrypted via HTTPS

🤖 Artificial Intelligence

The content of your conversations is sent to Anthropic API (Claude) to generate responses. Anthropic processes data according to its own privacy policy. Conversation content is not used to train AI models.

Important: Soleil is not a therapist or medical tool. In case of mental health crisis, contact a crisis helpline.

📤 Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We work only with:

  • Clerk — user authentication
  • Supabase — data storage
  • Anthropic — AI response generation
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Vercel — application hosting

⚖️ Your rights (GDPR)

  • Access — you can request a copy of your data
  • Rectification — you can correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — you can request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability — you can receive your data in electronic format
  • Objection — you can object to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us: adrianmalysa49@gmail.com

🍪 Cookies and localStorage

We use browser localStorage to remember your preferences (selected language, last mood date). We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.

👶 Children

Soleil is not intended for persons under 16 years of age. If you are under 16, please do not use the application.

📝 Policy changes

We will notify you of significant changes to this privacy policy through the application. Continued use of Soleil after changes implies acceptance.
